Our Values

We operate in baros all over the London and all over UK

Our core values are: trust, integrity, passion, stability, compassion, community,

Integrity

We believe that in order to be able to operate successfully, we must keep our moral principles and the belief that what we set out to achieve is appreciated by staff who appreciate and respect the company's integrity, vision, and values.

Community

For us, the community is important and we value the community from which our users come and that is why we want to offer them staff from their community who can offer them a care service at their home according to their needs and wishes.

Stability

We want to promote stability and therefore we want our staff to feel stable in our company. Stability is essential when we apply work practices to our staff and our service users. We are committed to maintaining effective communication with all service users, staff and partners/suppliers of our services.

Compassion

Compassion towards service users and colleagues is essential in our services and interactions with our staff. It is a driving force to have a successful service delivery. In order to achieve the success of the company, it is essential that our staff show compassion towards service users.

Pasion

When we offer our services to our service users, we do it with passion because passion is the basis of our company and our staff is led by the same passion that helps them offer a superior and quality service to our service users.

Trust

We want to develop a relationship based on trust with all service users, staff, health and social care professionals, commissioners and social services. We want to be honest, transparent in all the relationships we want to build so we can deliver value in what we do for service users, contributors and families.

Our Aims

Best Home Care Center with Love

We aim to be an outstanding care provider in the London Borough of Redbridge, Newham, Havering, District Council of Essex, and all other London Boroughs providing our clients with first class care, using every means available, to achieve the best possible outcomes for them.

At Get A Care Ltd the primary aims of our service are to offer consistent, person centred care for all service users in their individual house within an environment that encourages mutual respect, consideration for others, choice, independence, dignity, autonomy, individuality and trust.

Our Objectives

The objectives of Get A Care Ltd are to enable service users to enjoy a high quality of life by respecting six basic values:
Privacy, Independence, Rights, Dignity, Choice, Fulfilment, Security.

This is achieved by ensuring that all service users understand the concepts of:

Privacy and dignity and the ability to live in their own home whilst actively taking part in the services and facilities that is available to them.

To enable the service users to make choices and decisions about their own future.

To provide support and opportunities that will enable the service users to learn and develop their own skills.

To encourage the service users to have self-respect and to respect others including our staff.

To enable the service users to develop valued relationships with staff and members of the community.

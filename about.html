About Get A Care

Compassionate care with dignity, respect, and professionalism at its heart.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform home care services, Get A Care Ltd has grown from humble beginnings to become a trusted name in compassionate care across London and Essex.

Our journey began with a simple belief: everyone deserves to receive high-quality care in the comfort of their own home, with dignity and respect.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of clients with a team of dedicated professionals who share our commitment to excellence in care.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape everything we do

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, person-centered care that enables our clients to live independently in their own homes with dignity, choice, and fulfillment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most trusted and innovative home care provider, setting the standard for excellence in compassionate care across our communities.

Our Core Values

The foundation of our care philosophy

Compassion

We approach every interaction with genuine empathy and understanding, treating each client as we would our own family.

Respect

We honor the dignity, choices, and individuality of every person we serve, valuing their life experiences and preferences.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of care, continuously improving our services through training and innovation.

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency, building trust through ethical practices and open communication.

Community

We actively engage with and support the communities we serve, fostering connections and partnerships.

Empowerment

We support our clients' independence and help them maintain control over their lives and care decisions.
